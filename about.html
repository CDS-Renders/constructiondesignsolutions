The Background

Where Construction
Meets Design.

Construction Design Solutions was born out of a frustration I watched play out on job sites over and over again. A salesperson closes a deal, a customer signs a contract, and an installer shows up to build — and none of them are working from the same picture.

"Customers couldn't visualize what they bought. Installers had no reliable reference. Salespeople hoped for the best."

I spent years in the trades working kitchen and bathroom remodels. I saw firsthand how a single miscommunication could turn a profitable job into a change-order nightmare. The fix seemed obvious — give everyone a visual they can actually work from, before a single nail is driven.

Combining that hands-on construction experience with a mechanical engineering degree from Penn State, I built CDS around one idea: professional 3D visualization and construction documentation that serves every party in the remodeling process — not just the customer presentation, but the installer's reference document too.

That's the gap nobody else is filling. General freelance renderers make beautiful images but don't understand construction workflows. CDS sits in the middle — built by someone who has worked both sides — delivering at a price point that makes sense for every job.
